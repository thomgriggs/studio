Our Mission
mywear.house

We believe every garment you own deserves to be stored, tracked, and returned to you exactly as you remember it — pressed, photographed, and protected.

mywear.house was founded on a simple truth: great clothes deserve better than a crowded closet. We're building the infrastructure for modern wardrobes — climate-controlled warehouses, concierge delivery to high-rise buildings, and a digital inventory so complete you'll never forget what you own.

Process

How it works

01

Schedule Intake

Book a pickup window. We arrive with uniform cedar hangers and garment bags. Every piece is photographed — front, back, and 360° — under professional lighting before it ever leaves your home.

02

We Catalogue & Store

Your items are logged by color, brand, type, and season into your digital closet. We flag any stains or damage and route to our dry cleaning partners. Everything stored in our climate-controlled warehouse.

03

You Shop Your Own Closet

Browse your wardrobe from your phone. Queue pieces for delivery individually or build an outfit. Family members can add to the queue — you approve and schedule the delivery window.

04

We Deliver, Pressed

Garments arrive pressed and ready to wear — just like when you first bought them. Seasonal alerts let you know when to rotate. The more you use it, the smarter it gets.
