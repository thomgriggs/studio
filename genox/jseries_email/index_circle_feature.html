J-Series Pipe Shredders
Process large-diameter pipe with the Genox USA J-Series.
When your operation needs to process oversized plastic pipe, Genox USA has a J-Series shredder built for the job. From the J800 through the heavy-duty J2000, the lineup is designed for high-volume pipe recycling applications requiring strong cutting power, long pipe handling, and consistent material reduction.
The largest model, the J2000, can process plastic pipes and profiles up to 20 feet long and up to 72 inches in diameter, making it an ideal solution for large-diameter HDPE, PP, PVC, and other plastic pipe recycling applications.
