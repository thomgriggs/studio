Processing time varies depending on the type of transaction. Florida titles typically take 5-10 business days, while out-of-state titles can take 2-4 weeks. We offer expedited services for urgent needs and will provide you with a specific timeline for your situation during consultation.

Required documents vary by transaction type, but generally include: the current title or last registration, buyer and seller information, odometer disclosure, and bill of sale. For out-of-state titles, additional documentation may be required. We’ll provide you with a complete checklist specific to your transaction.

While we specialize in serving dealerships and wholesalers, we also assist individual sellers with complex title situations, particularly out-of-state transfers and duplicate title requests. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
